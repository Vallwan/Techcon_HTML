Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small community of technology enthusiasts and has grown into one of the most anticipated annual tech conferences in Africa. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of professionals, innovators, and students from around the world.

From its humble beginnings in a local university auditorium, TechCon now spans multiple days and venues, offering keynotes, workshops, and panel discussions that explore the latest trends and breakthroughs in technology.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower communities through technology education, networking, and innovation. We strive to bring together thought leaders, aspiring tech professionals, and visionary companies to drive real-world change.

By offering diverse sessions, inclusive programming, and hands-on experiences, we aim to bridge the digital divide and shape the future of tech in Africa and beyond.
